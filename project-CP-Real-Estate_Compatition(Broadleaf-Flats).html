Broadleaf Flats – Real Estate Senior Project

Project Overview

For my senior project I chose to compete in the Cal Poly Affordable Housing Competition. Together with a small team of related majors we created a 75 unit apartment building meant to be 100% affordable for the county of San Luis Obispo. This was challenging as there are specific requirements and guidelines that make affordable building more complex than market rate housing.

Project Details

Executive Summary

Broadleaf Flats is a 75-unit affordable housing development proposed for San Luis Obispo’s historic Little Italy neighborhood, designed to serve small families, young professionals, and low-income residents earning 20–60% of the Area Median Income. The 1.25-acre site at 2900 Broad St. offers studios, one-, two-, and three-bedroom units, with amenities including a central courtyard, daycare, community room, and on-site laundry. Prioritizing sustainability, the project uses mass timber construction, solar energy systems, and passive design strategies to reduce costs and environmental impact. With a total development cost of $55.6 million, Broadleaf Flats is financed through a blend of LIHTC equity, public grants, and affordable housing loans. The project supports walkable, transit-oriented living with direct access to SLO Transit and is expected to generate strong long-term financial performance with a projected DSCR of 1.15.

Key Project Stats

  • Total Units: 75 (74 affordable, 1 manager unit)
  • Unit Mix: 12 Studios (375 SF), 23 One-Bedrooms (450 SF), 20 Two-Bedrooms (750 SF), 20 Three-Bedrooms (900 SF)
  • Average Affordability: 37.33% AMI
  • Target Demographic: Small families, young professionals, seniors, and housing-insecure individuals
  • Construction Type: Mass timber (CLT & Glulam)
  • LEED Certification: Platinum
  • Amenities: Playground, community garden, picnic & BBQ areas, daycare, laundry room, community room, Wi-Fi
  • Total Development Cost: $55,619,053
  • Cost Per Unit: $741,587 | Cost Per SF: $1,162
  • Annual Revenue: $1,485,668 | Expenses: $638,315 | NOI: $774,069
  • DSCR: 1.15
  • LIHTC Equity: $21.2 million (9% credits at $0.85/credit)
